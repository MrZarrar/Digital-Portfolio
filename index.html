About Me

As a Computer Science undergraduate at Royal Holloway, University of London, I am driven by a passion for problem-solving and innovation. My academic journey is complemented by a diverse range of experiences, from leading technical projects to engaging in creative pursuits.

During my first year, I spearheaded a team to design and program an autonomous robot using the Lego Mindstorms EV3 platform. This project honed my skills in leadership, programming (Java, EV3Dev), and teamwork, culminating in the successful delivery of a functional robot that autonomously sorted objects by color—graded A+.

Beyond academics, I have honed my technical expertise as a freelance AI Trainer at Outlier AI, crafting challenging prompts and evaluating AI responses to refine cutting-edge systems. My role demands attention to detail, creativity, and adherence to rigorous rubrics, skills that I continually refine.

My passion for understanding complex systems extends beyond technology to the human body. Volunteering as a pharmacy assistant and participating in competitions like “Body in the Box” have taught me resilience, empathy, and adaptability—qualities I bring to all facets of my work.

Outside of academics and work, I channel my creativity into art, transitioning from traditional mediums to digital formats. This hobby has cultivated patience, independence, and innovative thinking.

I am always eager to explore new challenges, leveraging my skills in programming, AI training, and creative problem-solving to contribute meaningfully to technology and beyond.

Java Robot

Designed and built a Lego Mindstorms EV3 robot that autonomously identifies and sorts objects by color using ultrasonic, color, and sound sensors. Led the mechanical design and contributed to programming, focusing on behavior-based control and real-time object detection. This project strengthened my skills in robotics, problem-solving, and teamwork.

List 'N' Go Webapp

Developed a user-friendly grocery list app using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js (Express), with Playwright for data scraping. The app fetches real-time prices for items added to the list, helping users estimate costs and serving as a convenient checklist. Optimized for both desktop and mobile, it offers a smooth and intuitive experience.
